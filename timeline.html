The History of Music Timeline is an interactive web development project designed to explore over 1,500 years of musical history, from 500 AD to 2026.

The website presents key moments, innovations, composers, genres, and cultural developments in music through a horizontally scrollable timeline that allows users to travel through different historical periods in an engaging and intuitive way.

The project combines HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a dynamic user experience. Users can navigate through the timeline and interact with information boxes positioned at significant points in history.

Each event highlights an important contribution to the evolution of music, helping visitors understand how musical styles, technologies, and influential figures have shaped the music we know today.

A key feature of the website is its interactive design. A date marker follows the user's cursor, displaying the corresponding year on the timeline and providing a clearer sense of historical context while browsing.

The information boxes are intended to function as gateways to more detailed content, drawing inspiration from Wikipedia's approach to presenting accessible historical information.

The goal of this project was not only to demonstrate technical web development skills such as layout design, styling, user interaction, and JavaScript functionality, but also to create an educational resource that makes music history more engaging and easier to explore.

By combining visual storytelling with interactive elements, the website encourages users to discover the connections between different musical eras and appreciate the rich history of music across centuries.
